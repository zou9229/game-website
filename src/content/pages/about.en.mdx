## What Quest Codes Is

Quest Codes is an independently operated, fan-made Roblox codes and game guide website. We build focused references for players who want a direct answer, a visible checked date, and enough source context to decide what to try in the game.

The first editorial cluster covers 99 Nights in the Forest. It includes working and expired codes, crafting routes, classes, animals, maps, missing children, gems, badges, survival decisions, and update notes. We expand only when a new page answers a distinct player question and can be supported by reliable public information.

Quest Codes is not affiliated with Roblox Corporation or any Roblox game developer. Roblox, game names, thumbnails, and related marks belong to their respective owners.

## Our Editorial Approach

Game data changes quickly, so every page should make its evidence and limitations understandable. Our workflow prioritizes:

- Official Roblox game pages and public developer announcements.
- Established gaming publications and public reference wikis.
- Clearly labeled checked dates and source links.
- Conservative wording when sources disagree.
- Separate active, special, conflicting, and expired code states.
- Corrections instead of silently preserving outdated routes.

We do not publish invented code rewards, hidden formulas, drop rates, tier values, crafting costs, or patch claims. Recommendations may be included when the reasoning is visible, but they are not presented as confirmed game data.

For the complete process, read our [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy).

## Updates And Corrections

Codes and public update metadata receive the highest monitoring priority because they can change faster than normal guide information. High-risk guide topics such as tier lists, crafting details, animal behavior, and map routes remain subject to human review before factual changes are published.

When a trusted source changes or a player reports a possible error, we recheck the relevant claim. If sources still disagree, the page should show that uncertainty rather than force a definitive answer.

## Advertising Independence

Quest Codes may use Google AdSense and may later include clearly disclosed affiliate links. Advertising does not determine whether a code is marked active, how a class is evaluated, which source is cited, or whether a correction is published.

Ads and sponsored links must remain distinguishable from navigation, copy buttons, game links, and editorial recommendations. For more information about advertising cookies and data use, read our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

## Contact Quest Codes

Players, developers, rights holders, and advertisers can reach us through the [Contact page](/contact). Useful reports include the page URL, the exact statement that may be wrong, and a public source that can be checked.
