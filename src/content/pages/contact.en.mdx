## Email

Email Quest Codes at [contact@questcodes.com](mailto:contact@questcodes.com).

We aim to review normal messages within five business days. Time-sensitive code or guide corrections are prioritized when the message includes enough information to verify the claim.

## Report A Code Or Guide Correction

For the fastest review, include:

- The Quest Codes page URL.
- The exact code, sentence, table entry, or route that may be outdated.
- What happened when you tested it, including platform or server details when relevant.
- A public developer post, Roblox page, publication, wiki entry, screenshot, or video that supports the correction.
- The date and time you checked the information.

Community reports are useful signals, but a report is not automatically published as fact. We compare it with available sources and keep public wording conservative when reliable sources disagree.

## Copyright And Rights Requests

Quest Codes uses game-related names, thumbnails, screenshots, and video references for identification, commentary, navigation, or explanation. Rights holders may request a correction, attribution change, link update, or removal by emailing us.

Please identify the material, provide the page URL, explain your relationship to the rights holder, and describe the requested action. We may ask for reasonable verification before changing or removing material.

## Privacy And Advertising

For privacy questions, include the relevant page or feature but do not send passwords, authentication secrets, API keys, payment details, or other sensitive credentials.

For advertising or business inquiries, clearly label the message as an advertising inquiry. Paid relationships do not control code status, source selection, guide conclusions, or correction decisions.

Before contacting us, you may also review our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy), [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service), and [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy).
