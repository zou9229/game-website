## Introduction

Quest Codes is a fan-made game codes and guide website. We publish Roblox game codes, guide pages, tier lists, and related reference content. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the site.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- Basic usage data, such as pages visited, browser type, device type, and approximate region.
- Information you voluntarily provide if account features, comments, forms, or email alerts are enabled later.
- Cookies or similar technologies used for preferences, analytics, security, and site performance.

We do not need sensitive personal information to provide public guide pages.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Operate, secure, and improve the website.
- Understand which pages and game guides are useful.
- Detect abuse, spam, or technical issues.
- Respond to user requests when contact features are available.

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, security, and search indexing. These providers may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

## Advertising and Affiliate Links

Quest Codes may use Google AdSense and may include clearly disclosed affiliate links. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, measure, personalize, limit, and protect advertising.

Google's use of advertising cookies may allow Google and its partners to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to Quest Codes or other websites. Visitors can manage or opt out of personalized advertising through [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/). Visitors may also review industry opt-out choices at [AboutAds](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

For visitors in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, Quest Codes will use an appropriate consent flow when required before personalized advertising cookies are used.

Affiliate links do not change our code status, tier recommendations, source labels, or editorial conclusions. When a link can earn Quest Codes a commission, the commercial relationship will be disclosed near the recommendation or link.
